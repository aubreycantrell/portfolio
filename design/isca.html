ISCA Website Redesign

Frontend Brand System Navigation Events Subscriptions

Design aspects and content for homepage and event pages of International Swim Coaches Association

Overview & Goals

Through ISCA's mission to educate and unite the global swim coach community, the main task in the redesign of the website was to make valuable coaching resources more accessible, as well as advertise ISCA's meets and workshops more broadly. We aimed to streamline registration processes by condensing steps and conveying necessary information in concise ways, and advertise ISCA's socials for swimmers, coaches and parents to easily access.

Brand System

We started by establishing a brand-wide font and company colors to create a cohesive, minimal system with high contrast. We chose the sans serif typeface Hanken Grotesk to convey a smart, bold tone. These choices informed the UI components and refreshed logo.

Navigation

Header

The header groups core resources into clear clusters and uses contrasting brand colors to emphasize selections. This improves first-click discoverability and helps new visitors form a quick mental model.

Footer

The footer extends the header’s structure and surfaces a clear communication channel and ISCA’s primary offerings for dependable wayfinding.

Advertising Events

Landing

A dynamic carousel showcases upcoming events using AI-generated backgrounds paired with pre-existing event logos. This creates an immediate, branded focal point for visitors.

Testimonials

A simple, linear layout for testimonials increases credibility and reduces cognitive load for scanning.

Events Bar

Bright, contrasting tiles with subtle shadows encourage interaction. A hover-zoom affordance previews details without a click.

Socials & Subscriptions

Newsletter Pop-up

A lightweight modal focuses attention on joining the newsletter without obstructing browsing for long.

Email & YouTube

One-click subscription paths lower friction and increase repeat engagement across channels.

Services

Prominent service cards with hover zoom help coaches quickly find relevant offerings.

Instagram Feed

A live feed provides social proof and current activity; tiles link directly to Instagram.

Membership

A concise value summary with a real photo and clear CTA increases conversion to join.
