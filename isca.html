ISCA Website Redesign

Design aspects and content for homepage and event pages of International Swim Coaches Association

Through ISCA's mission to educate and unite the global swim coach community, the main task in the redesign of the website was to make valubale coaching resources more accessible, as well as advertise ISCA's meets and workshops more broadly. We aimed to streamline registration processes by condensing steps and conveying necessary information in concise ways, and advertising ISCA's socials for swimmers, coaches and parents to easily access.

We started off by establishing a brand wide font and company colors to create a cohesive and consistent appearance. This allowed for a more minimalistic look with fewer, but highly contrasting colors. We established a sans serif typeface, Hanken Grotesk, to be the main font used to create a clean look that conveys a smart, bold brand. These new colors and fonts created the base of the website redesign and were implemented into the logo.

We then focused in on three main goals within the website: we wanted to streamline the navigation for accessibility purposes, prioritize advertizing events and workshops, and increase visability of their socials and subscriptions.
