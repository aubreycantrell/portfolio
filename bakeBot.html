BakeBot

Your new Baking Assistant: An Interactive Voice Skill intergrated with AI making the baking process easier and more efficient

The baking recommendation tool and assistant is designed to simplify the baking process by enable people to bake dishes more efficiently and effectively without significant concern for complex details and messes. Using a voice system, it allows users to find, customize, and follow recipes based on dietary needs, time limits, and available ingredients, all without touching a device. The system offers tailored recipe recommendations, with the flexibility to adjust for ingredient substitutions, and provides step-by-step guidance that users can follow at their own pace. Built-in timers, the option to ask clarifying questions, and the ability to go back to previous steps further enhance the user experience, making baking easier and more intuitive.
