Design Inquires

Through my portfolio I hope to convey both my passions for interface interactions and artistic inqiries, and how they can be combined to support intruiging design concepts. With these goals, I brought over a few of my art pieces to incorporate into the website, to use both as functionality and aesthetic purposes.

With my use of clean lines, blocking and contrasting colors, I hope to create an experience that immerses you in my art, but also helps you better understand by eye for interface design.
