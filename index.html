Aubrey Cantrell

Computer Science and Studio Arts @ Wellesley College

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a junior at Wellesley College, double majoring in Computer Science and Studio Arts, and I am passionate about finding the balance between artistic and purposeful designs, to better user interaction and creating memorable interface experiences.

Originally from the Bay Area, CA, as well as living in Hong Kong and Nanjing, I have found value in diverse approaches to concepts and ideas, and always look to incorporate them in my work. I love to be outdoors; you can find me surfing, rockclimbing, cycling, or hiking!

Behind the Design

Through my portfolio I hope to convey both my passions for interface interactions and artistic inquiries, and how they can be combined to support intruiging design concepts. With these goals, I incorporated a few of my art pieces into the website, to use both for functionality and aesthetic purposes.

With my use of clean lines, blocking and contrasting colors, I hope to create an experience that immerses you in my art, but also helps you better understand my eye for interface design.
