100 Cranes, Revisited

A reflection piece on my 100th day of school, in 2010, where each student brought in 100 objects to represent each day of school. My family through a labor of love, helped me fold 100 cranes. These years later, my family's support of my education and passion for arts persists, and these imprints of 100 cranes represent their dedication to my flourishing.

Lithography 2023
